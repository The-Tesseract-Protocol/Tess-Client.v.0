Core Features

Built on privacy-first principles to ensure your financial autonomy and data security.

Private Deposits

Deposit funds into a shared pool with a private withdrawal secret only you control. Your deposit is verified on-chain, but your identity is never exposed — giving you full authority over your funds.

End-to-End Encryption

Your payment instructions are protected by dual-layer encryption. Each party in the process only sees what they need to — no single entity ever has the full picture of your transaction.

Confidential Distribution

Your recipients get paid without any traceable link back to you. Payments settle on-chain for trust and finality, but the connection between sender and receiver stays completely private.
